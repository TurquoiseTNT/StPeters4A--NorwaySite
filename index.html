Welcome To Seb's Site On Norway!

Population

5.4 Million People Live In Norway as of 2020.

Capital

Oslo Is Norway's Capital City With Just Over 1 Million People.

Names

Real Name: Kingdom of Norway.

Short Name: Norway.

Norweigan Name: Norge.

Government

Norway's Government is Known as the Constitutional Monarchy.

Language

People In Norway speak Norweigan.

Religion

Most People In Norway are Christians.

Currency

Norway's Currency Is Called Norweigan Krone (NOK).

National Symbols

Norway's Symbols are a moose, a flower and the coat of arms.

National Colours

Red, White and Blue.

National Anthem

Norway's National Anthem Is Ja, vi elsker dette landet (Yes, we love this country)

National Day

Norway's National Day is The 17th of May.
